White Sauce Pasta

This white sauce pasta is an easy weeknight meal that’s ready under 30 minutes. You will love this rich, creamy & saucy pasta, perfectly seasoned with Italian herbs & topped with red chili flakes. It’s lighter and not heavy and is a satisfying comfort food. White sauce pasta is simply cooked pasta mixed with a silky smooth & decadent white sauce made of milk, butter and flour. White sauce also known as Bechamel sauce has its origin from the Italian & French Cuisines.

Prep Time

15 minutes

Cooking Time

30 minutes

Servings

4 People

Procedure

Step1 –
Take a big vessel and add pasta water salt and a 1 tbspn oil.
Step2 –
cook the pasta on a medium flame.
Step3 –
While the pasta is being cooked take a pan add oil and heat it on a medium flame.
Step4 –
Add finely chopped garlic, salt and black pepper and stir well till the garlic turns brown.
Step5 –
Add all the veggies( black bell pepper yellow bell pepper and capsicum) stirr well on a low flame.
Step5 –
Close the lid and cook the veggies for 4 to 5 mins on a medium flame. You can add more vegetables (mushrooms carrots etc) if you want.
Step6 –
Remove the lid stirr well and check if all the veggies are nicely cooked. In between stirr the pasta a bit to avoid it from getting over– cooked.
Step7 –
Store this in a bowl and let it rest for 5 mins.
Step8 –
once the pasta is cooked drain the water and cool the pasta in a dish. Keep some water for further use.
Step9 –
In the same pan add butter and maida and mix well. Make sure you dont get any lumps and stirr well.
Step10 –
Gradually add milk and stirr continuously to avoid lumps.
Step11 –
Add the cheese and mix well for 2 mins
Step12 –
Once you get a thick consistency add oregano and chilliflakes 1 tspn each
Step13 –
Add the cooked veggies and stirr well for 2 mins.
Step14 –
Add the boil pasta and stirr well for another 2 to 3 mins.
Step15 –
Grate cheese on top.
Step16 –
close the lid and cook it for 2 mins on a low flame.
Step17 –
open the lid mix well and turn off the gas.
Step18 –
Add a bit or oregano and chilliflakes on top and serve. (Optional)

Ingredients

Garlic –
15 to 20 cloves (finely chopped)
Black pepper–
1 1/2 tbspn
Salt –
add Acc to taste
Capsicum –
1 (finely chopped)
Yellow bell pepper –
1 (finely chopped)
Red bell pepper –
1 (finely chopped)
Pasta–
250 gm
Maida –
2 tbspn
Butter –
3 tbspn (incase of maida and butter adjust quantity)
Milk –
2 cups (depends and the amount of white sauce required)
Cheese –
2 cubes (grated)
Oil –
3 1/5 tbsp
Oregano –
1 tbpsn
Chilliflakes–
1 tbpsn

Tip –

If the pasta is overcooked or sticks to the pan add the drained water and stirr well.
